1.2. PURPOSE OF THE REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
ABC is embarking on a journey to act on our digital strategy in delivering exceptional experience, is working on an initiative to enhance the efficiency, flexibility, and collaborative potential of our digital content and asset process enabling a wider range of team members to easily update and manage web content, including digital assets.
The new Web Content Management (WCM) and Digital Asset Management (DAM) Solution are required to support ABC’s digital strategy in delivering exceptional experience.
The adoption of a new WCM and DAM Solution is aimed at streamlining our content update, reuse, and approval processes, reducing reliance on specialized technical skills, and fostering a more dynamic and responsive digital presence.
The purpose of this RFP is to select a best-of-suite hybrid WCM solution and an easy to use, integrated DAM Solutions, that aligns with ABC’s requirements with an implementation Vendor to assist with implementation of the solution. ABC is also interested in acquiring optional sustainment services for the best-of-suite solution.
ABC takes a strong approach to network security as it’s always of critical and paramount importance to maintain the safety of our corporate data. Forefront to that, ABC needs the ability to understand, prioritize, respond to, and protect our digital assets from constantly evolving cyber security vulnerabilities.
For detailed scope and requirements please see section 4 Purpose and Scope.

4. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
4.1. BACKGROUND AND CONTEXT
4.1.1. OUR VIEW OF THE CHALLENGE
Currently, the digital experience team at ABC manages all content changes and edits on our public website using hard coding or WordPress. We share our digital assets from an internal shared file location. While this approach has served us thus far, it cannot fully support ABC's goal of delivering an intuitive, personalized, and exceptional customer experience on an ongoing basis.
ABC is committed to enhancing the digital customer experience. We aim to create customer experiences that are not only intuitive and personalized but also exceptional, aligning with the pace of market demands. This will help us increase trust, loyalty, and retention, while also reducing the cost to serve.
However, with the increasing workload, our current approach restricts the ability to make quick and adaptable content modifications or share digital assets across the various stakeholders that use them. This limitation affects our flexibility, efficiency, and the ability to reuse content or assets, ultimately hindering our ability to respond swiftly to market changes and customer needs.
4.1.2. BUSINESS GOAL AND OBJECTIVES
The business goal of this initiative is to enhance ABC’s ability to achieve our strategic objective of delivering exceptional experiences by leveraging WCM and DAM solution that meets our requirements.
The business objectives of acquiring these two technologies are to:
a) Improve content management processes for our websites and digital channels.
b) Improve shareability of digital assets across stakeholders.
c) Empower team members to create, post and edit content easily.
d) Provide a central repository to support omnichannel content management and asset use.
e) Customize content and experiences for individual users based on their behavior, location, preferences, and other data.
Alberta Blue Cross Request for Proposal | Web Content Management and Digital Asset Management Solution 13
4.1.3. EXPECTED OUTCOMES
The expected outcomes of the WCM and DAM solution are twofold, offering both internal and external benefits. Internally, the digital experience team will achieve more efficient use of front-end developers, allowing them to concentrate on other tasks rather than content management. The Content Team will experience quicker, more agile content updates, seamless integration with MarTech tools, and improved brand consistency across digital channels. Additionally, this solution helps to minimize auditing and compliance risks, ensuring a more reliable content management process.
Externally, ABC’s customers will benefit from a more intuitive, personalized, and seamless digital experience, leading to increased satisfaction and engagement.
In summary, a successfully implemented Solution will achieve the stated business outcomes, deliver the functional, non-functional, and integration requirements, and will resolve known issues and pain points. The Solution, including integrations, will be tested, and implemented into an acceptable operational production environment. The Solution will be stable, secure, operate effectively, and meet all acceptance criteria and required business, technical, data, and security standards. End users and technical support staff will be trained as required and supporting documentation will be developed.

4.3. SCOPE
4.3.1. SOFTWARE SOLUTION SCOPE
The Functional Requirements of the WCM and DAM Solution have been developed and captured in Response Form B-Functional Requirements. Those requirements are categorized into Features described below. FEATURE Description
Content and Asset Authoring and Management
The ability to create, edit, organize, and manage digital content and assets, including text, images, and multimedia, within a centralized platform.
Usability
Ensures that the system is easy to use, with an intuitive interface and straightforward navigation, enabling non-technical users to manage content and assets efficiently.
Workflow Previewing and Publishing
Features that allow content creators to preview their work in various formats (e.g., desktop, mobile) and manage the approval process before publishing content live.
Administration
Functionalities that enable system administrators to manage users, permissions, settings, and overall system configuration to ensure smooth operation and security.
Multi-experience Delivery
The ability to deliver and optimize content across multiple digital touchpoints, such as websites, mobile apps and social media.
Personalization
The ability to deliver tailored content and experiences based on individual user preferences, behaviors, and interactions. This includes the ability to surface personalized content in the user interface.
Digital Asset Management
The ability to handle, store, and publish any format of multimedia and content assets. It provides a centralized repository with metadata tagging for easy asset discovery, along with user access controls and collaboration features.
